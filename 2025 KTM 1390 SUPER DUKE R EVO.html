2025 KTM 1390 Super Duke R EVO

The KTM 1390 SUPER DUKE R EVO is truly an evolved BEAST. Not only does it share the same advancement as the all-new KTM 1390 SUPER DUKE R, but it also brings intuitive WP Semi-Active Suspension Technology (SAT) to the fore. Being able to automatically adapt its suspension to the rider's input or the road surface, it brings intelligence to the tarmac and into the hunt. This motorcycle is designed, developed, and produced in Austria.

  • CO2 emissions: 139 g/km
