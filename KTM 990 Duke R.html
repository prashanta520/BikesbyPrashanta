KTM 990 Duke R

The KTM 990 DUKE R steps things up to new levels of authority. Named THE PUNISHER, its intensions are clear to set the benchmark for the competition. Boasting the highest torque figure in its class, it is the most READY TO RACE mid-weight Naked bike ever produced out of Mattighofen, Austria.

Technical Details

Engine

  • Torque: 103 Nm
  • Transmission 6-speed
  • Battery capacity 10 Ah
  • Cooling Liquid cooled with water/oil heat exchanger
  • Displacement: 947 cm³

Chassis

  • Weight (without fuel): 179 kg
  • Tank capacity (approx.): 23 l
  • Wheelbase: 1577 mm
  • Front brake disc diameter: 320 mm
  • Rear brake disc diameter: 240 mm
  • Front brake: 2x radially mounted 4 piston caliperd caliper, brake disc Ø 267 mm
  • Rear Break: Single-piston floating calliper, brake disc
  • Chain: 525 X-Ring
  • Frame design: Chromium-Molybdenum-Steel frame using the engine as stressed element, powder coated
  • Front suspension: WP APEX 48
  • Ground clearance: 210 mm
  • Rear suspension: WP APEX PRO 7746
  • Seat height: 840 mm
  • Steering head angle: 65.9 °
  • Rear subframe design: Aluminium, powder coated
  • Suspension travel (front): 220 mm
  • Suspension travel (rear): 220 mm
  • Tyre dimensions (front/rear): 90/90; 150/70
  • Wheels: Spoked wheels with aluminium rims
  • Wheelbase: 1481 ± 15 mm
