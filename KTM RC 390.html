KTM RC 390

At the top end of the line-up, the 2024 KTM RC 390 receives two new eye-catching color schemes, with orange-on-blue and orange-on-black options. Both receive striking, signature orange frames, with the orange-on-black getting orange rims.

Technical Details

Engine

  • Torque: 37 Nm
  • Transmission: 6-speed
  • Cooling: Liquid cooled
  • Power in kW: 22 kW
  • Starter: Electric starter
  • Stroke: 60 mm
  • Bore: 89 mm
  • Clutch: PASC™ anti-hopping clutch, mechanically operated
  • CO2 Emissions: 81 g/km
  • Displacement: 373 cm³
  • EMS: Bosch EMS with RBW
  • Design: 1-cylinder, 4-stroke engine
  • Fuel Consumption: 3.46 l/100 km
  • Fuel-Mixture Generation: Bosch EFI
  • Lubrication: Wet sump

Chassis

  • Weight (without fuel): 158 kg
  • Tank Capacity (approx.): 13.7 l
  • Wheelbase: 1343 mm
  • ABS: Bosch 9.1 MP Two Channel ABS
  • Front Brake Disc Diameter: 320 mm
  • Rear Brake Disc Diameter: 230 mm
  • Front Brake: Four-piston radial fixed caliper, brake disc
  • Rear Brake: Single-piston floating caliper, brake disc
  • Chain: 520 X-ring
  • Frame Design: Steel trellis frame, powder coated
  • Front Suspension: WP APEX 43
  • Ground Clearance: 158 mm
  • Handlebar: Steel clip-on handlebars
  • Rear Suspension: WP APEX - Monoshock
  • Seat Height: 824 mm
  • Silencer: Stainless steel primary and aluminum secondary silencer
